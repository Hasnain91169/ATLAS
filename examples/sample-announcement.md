# Company-Wide Announcement: Q3 Reorganization at Nimbus Software

**From:** Priya Raman, CEO
**To:** All Nimbus Software employees (420 staff)
**Date:** Next Monday

Team,

I'm writing to share a significant reorganization that will take effect at the
start of Q3. This affects how several departments are structured, and I want to
be direct about what is changing and why.

## What is changing

- The **Platform Engineering** and **Infrastructure** teams (currently 60 engineers
  across two reporting lines) will merge into a single **Core Systems** group led
  by **Marcus Feld**, VP of Engineering. Team leads **Aisha Khan** and **Tom Berg**
  will now both report to Marcus.
- The **Customer Success** organization (35 people under **Dana Lopez**) will split
  into **Onboarding** and **Retention**, with Dana moving to lead Retention and a
  new director to be hired for Onboarding.
- **Data Science** (12 people) moves out of the Product org and into a new
  centralized **AI & Insights** function reporting directly to the CTO, **Sanjay Rao**.
- Roughly **18 roles** in middle management are being consolidated. Affected
  managers have been contacted individually by HR (**Elena Ward**, Head of People)
  and will be offered either new individual-contributor roles or a transition package.

## Why we are doing this

Over the last year, duplicated tooling between Platform and Infrastructure has slowed
releases, and customers have told us onboarding and long-term support need different
kinds of attention. Centralizing Data Science lets us invest in shared AI capabilities
instead of scattered per-team efforts. We expect these changes to shorten our release
cycle and improve customer retention, which has dipped from 91% to 86% this year.

## What this means for you

Most individual contributors will keep their current role, manager, and compensation.
If your team or reporting line is changing, your lead will meet with you this week.
The 18 affected managers have already been notified. No changes to salaries or benefits
are being made as part of this reorganization, and we are not reducing overall headcount.

I know reorganizations create uncertainty. Elena and I will host an all-hands Q&A on
Thursday, and managers across every team are available for one-on-ones. Please bring
your questions — hard ones included.

— Priya
